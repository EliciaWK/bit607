The
Haukai Restaurant

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands.
We want customers to experience Te Ao Māori with the use of karakia for blessing food and other Māori culture elements.
We started this Restaurant because we are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area.

The restaurant is only open in the evenings at the moment, from Wednesday to Sunday inclusive.
The dinner menu features Māori and Pākeha kai options and drinks. Navigate to our Menu page to check out the different cruisines we have to offer!
